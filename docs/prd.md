# 1. Executive Summary

Afghan Tappeti is a premium bilingual e-commerce and branding platform specializing in authentic hand-knotted Oriental, Persian, and Afghan rugs.

The platform serves two equally important purposes:

1. Luxury Brand Presence
2. Online Product Sales

Afghan Tappeti is not simply an online store. It is a digital showroom that communicates craftsmanship, heritage, authenticity, and timeless luxury while enabling customers to discover, explore, and purchase high-quality rugs.

The website will support:

- English
- Italian

Target market:

- Italy
- Europe

The platform must create trust, emotional connection, and an elegant shopping experience.

---

# 2. Vision Statement

To become one of Europe's most trusted and recognized destinations for authentic hand-knotted Oriental, Persian, and Afghan rugs by combining heritage, craftsmanship, and modern digital experiences.

---

# 3. Mission Statement

Afghan Tappeti connects customers with authentic handmade rugs that bring warmth, beauty, culture, and timeless elegance into their homes.

Each rug is more than decoration—it is a unique work of art with its own history and character.
