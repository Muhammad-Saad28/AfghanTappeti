Since 1924

A Legacy of Knots

Afghan Tappeti was born from a passion for the loom and a commitment to preserving the nomadic heritage of Central Asia. Our journey began in the heart of Herat, where the scent of natural dyes and the rhythmic sound of beating combs filled the air.

Today, we bridge the gap between ancient craftsmanship and contemporary luxury, sourcing directly from weaving families to ensure both quality and fair compensation for those whose hands create these timeless treasures.
